## Quick Reference

**What's FREE:**
✓ Community, Swap Meet, All Tools, Track Locations, Events

**What's PAID:**
$ Setup Sheets only (save & sync)

**Pricing:**
- Basic: $9.99/month or $99.99/year
- Premium: $12.99/month or $134.99/year
- Free Trial: 7 days on all plans

**Product IDs:**
- iOS: com.pitbox.[tier].[interval]
