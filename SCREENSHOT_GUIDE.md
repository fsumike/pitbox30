## üé® Design Guidelines for Text Overlays

### Typography
**Headline (Top Text):**
- Font: Bold, Sans-serif (Arial Black, Helvetica Bold, Montserrat Bold)
- Size: 72-96pt
- Color: White with dark stroke/shadow OR solid dark color
- Alignment: Center

**Subtext (Bottom Text):**
- Font: Regular or Medium Sans-serif
- Size: 48-60pt
- Color: Gray or light color
- Alignment: Center
- Use bullet points (‚Ä¢) to separate phrases

### Layout
**Safe Areas:**
- Top overlay: 200px from top
- Bottom overlay: 200px from bottom
- Side margins: 100px on each side

**Background Treatment:**
- Add semi-transparent overlay bar behind text (black at 40% opacity)
- OR use drop shadow on text for readability
- Ensure high contrast between text and background

### Brand Colors
- Primary: Black (#000000)
- Accent: Gold (#FFD700) - Use sparingly for highlights
- Background overlays: Dark gray (#1a1a1a) at 40-60% opacity

## üõ†Ô∏è Tools to Create Screenshots

### Option 1: Device Screenshots + Canva (EASIEST)
1. **Capture screenshots on actual device**
   - iOS: Volume Up + Side button
   - Android: Volume Down + Power button

1. **Use iOS Simulator** (Xcode) or **Android Emulator**
2. **Set device to exact screenshot dimensions**
3. **Capture screenshots** (Cmd+S on iOS Simulator)
4. **Open in Photoshop**
5. **Add text layers** with proper fonts and effects
6. **Export as PNG** at 100% quality

### Option 3: Screenshot.one or Figma (DESIGN-FOCUSED)
1. **Create artboard** with exact dimensions
2. **Place your app screenshot**
3. **Add device frame** (optional but nice)
4. **Add text overlays**
5. **Export at 2x or 3x** for retina displays

## üéØ Pro Tips for Great Screenshots

### 1. Use Real Data, Not Lorem Ipsum
- Use actual track names (Eldora, Knoxville, Williams Grove)
- Real car numbers (25, 1A, 51)
- Believable lap times
- Proper racing terminology

### 2. Show, Don't Tell
- Screenshots should demonstrate features
- Users should understand the app at a glance
- Less text overlay is often better
- Let the interface speak for itself

### 3. Consistent Lighting and Style
- All screenshots in same mode (light or dark)
- Consistent time of day in status bar
- Same battery level and signal (looks cleaner)
- Remove notification badges

### 4. Think Mobile-First
- Text must be readable on small screens
- High contrast is critical
- Simple, bold fonts work best
- Don't overcrowd with information

### 5. Test on Actual Devices
- What looks good on desktop may not translate
- View screenshots on phone before submitting
- Check readability at actual size
- Get feedback from real users

## üö´ Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå **Using Low Resolution Images**
- Always use exact dimensions required
- Don't upscale smaller screenshots
- Blurry images get rejected

‚ùå **Too Much Text Overlay**
- Keep it simple and scannable
- 1-2 short phrases maximum
- Users won't read paragraphs

‚ùå **Inconsistent Branding**
- Use same fonts across all screenshots
- Maintain color consistency
- Professional appearance matters

‚ùå **Showing Errors or Bugs**
- No loading spinners
- No error messages
- No empty states (unless intentional)

‚ùå **Poor Contrast**
- Light text on light background = invisible
- Test in both light and dark environments
- Status bar should always be visible

‚ùå **Generic or Boring Content**
- "Lorem ipsum" looks unprofessional
- Stock photos are obvious
- Make it look like a real racing app

## üì± App Preview Video (Optional but Recommended)

### iOS App Preview Specs
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080 (landscape) or 1080 x 1920 (portrait)
- Duration: 15-30 seconds
- Format: .mov, .m4v, or .mp4
- Size: Under 500MB

### Android Promo Video Specs
- Upload to YouTube first
- Duration: 30 seconds - 2 minutes
- Orientation: Landscape preferred
- Resolution: 1080p minimum

### Video Content Structure (30 seconds)
```
0:00-0:03 - App logo splash with racing sound
0:03-0:10 - Setup sheet creation (fast forward)
0:10-0:15 - Quick tool usage montage
0:15-0:20 - Community feed scroll
0:20-0:25 - "Works Offline" message + demo
0:25-0:30 - Logo + tagline + "Download Now"
```

### Screen Recording Tools
- **iOS:** Built-in screen recording (Settings ‚Üí Control Center)
- **Android:** Built-in screen recording (swipe down, tap icon)
- **Mac:** QuickTime Player (File ‚Üí New Screen Recording)
- **Editing:** iMovie, Premiere Rush, CapCut (all free options)
