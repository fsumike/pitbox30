## Why This Model?

1. **Community First**: We want everyone to connect and share racing knowledge

3. **Marketplace Access**: Buy and sell parts without barriers
4. **Setup Privacy**: Your competitive setups are what you pay to protect and manage

> "Subscription required for setup sheets only. Community, Swap Meet, and all Tools are FREE for everyone!"
