WHAT THIS DOES
===============================================

Your package.json now has a new script called "build:ci" that:

1. Adds Android platform (npx cap add android)
2. Builds your web app (vite build)
3. Syncs to Android (npx cap sync android)

All automatic! No more "platform not added" error.

===============================================
WHY IT WAS FAILING
===============================================

Your .gitignore excludes the android/ folder, so it's not in Git.
The CI/CD runner needs that folder to build.

This fix generates the android/ folder automatically during build.
Problem solved!
