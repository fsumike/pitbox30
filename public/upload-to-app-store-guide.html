Part 1: Build Your App with Capawesome

1
Open Terminal/Command Prompt

On Mac:

  1. Press Command + Space
  2. Type "Terminal"
  3. Press Enter

On Windows:

  1. Press Windows Key
  2. Type "Command Prompt"
  3. Press Enter
2
Navigate to Your Project Folder

Type this command (replace with your actual folder location):

cd /path/to/your/project

Example:

cd /Users/yourname/Desktop/pitbox-project
3
Build with Capawesome

Copy and paste this exact command:

npm run capawesome:build:ios

Press Enter and wait. This takes 5-10 minutes.

‚ö†Ô∏è Don't close the Terminal window while it's building!
4
Download Your .ipa File from Capawesome

When the build finishes, you'll see a link in the Terminal output.

  1. Look for text that says "Build completed" or "Download your app"
  2. You'll see a link like: https://cloud.capawesome.io/...
  3. Copy that link and open it in your browser
  4. Click "Download .ipa"
  5. Save the file (remember where you save it!)
üí° Alternative: Go to cloud.capawesome.io, log in, and find your latest build under "Builds" ‚Üí "iOS"

Part 2: Upload to App Store Connect

5
Go to App Store Connect
  1. Open your browser
  2. Go to: https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
  3. Sign in with your Apple Developer account
6
Find Your App
  1. Click on "My Apps"
  2. Find and click on "PitBox" (your app name)
7
Use Transporter App to Upload
‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: You CANNOT upload the .ipa directly through the website. You MUST use the Transporter app.

Download Transporter:

  1. Open the Mac App Store
  2. Search for "Transporter"
  3. Download and install it (it's free from Apple)
Windows Users: You need access to a Mac to upload. Ask a friend with a Mac, use a Mac at a library, or use a Mac rental service online.
8
Upload with Transporter
  1. Open the Transporter app
  2. Sign in with your Apple Developer account
  3. Click the + Add App button
  4. Find and select your .ipa file (the one you downloaded from Capawesome)
  5. Click Deliver
  6. Wait for upload to complete (5-15 minutes depending on your internet speed)
9
Wait for Processing

After upload completes:

  1. Go back to App Store Connect in your browser
  2. Click on your app (PitBox)
  3. Go to TestFlight or App Store tab
  4. You'll see "Processing" next to your build
  5. Wait 15-60 minutes for Apple to process it
üìß You'll get an email when processing is done
10
Submit for Review

Once processing is complete:

  1. Go to App Store Connect
  2. Click your app
  3. Click "+ Version" or select your existing version
  4. Scroll down to "Build"
  5. Click the + button next to Build
  6. Select your NEW build (it will have today's date)
  7. Scroll to the bottom
  8. Click Submit for Review

‚úÖ Done!

Apple will review your app in 1-3 days. You'll get an email when it's approved.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Q: I don't have a Mac. How do I upload?

A: You MUST use a Mac for Transporter. Options:

  • Ask a friend with a Mac
  • Use a Mac at an Apple Store or library
  • Rent a Mac online (services like MacInCloud)
  • Hire someone on Fiverr to do it for you ($5-$20)

Q: Capawesome build failed

A: Run this command first:

npm run build

Then try the Capawesome build again.

Q: I can't find my .ipa file

A: Go to cloud.capawesome.io, sign in, click "Builds" ‚Üí "iOS" ‚Üí find your latest build ‚Üí "Download"

Q: Upload is taking forever

A: .ipa files are 50-200MB. On slow internet, this can take 30+ minutes. Be patient.
