Share racing content from any social media app directly to Pit Community

1

Find Content

Browse Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or any social media app and find a cool race car video or post you want to share with Pit Community.

2

Tap Share

Tap the Share button on the post (usually a paper airplane or share icon). You'll see PitBox appear in your share menu alongside other apps.

3

Select PitBox

Tap the PitBox icon from the share menu. The PitBox app will open automatically with a beautiful preview of what you're sharing.

4

Add Your Thoughts

Add your own comment to the post, use emojis, and see a preview of the original content with platform-specific branding.

5

Share to Community

Tap "Share to Community" and your post instantly appears in the Pit Community feed with "Shared from [Platform]" attribution.

6

Engage

Your shared post appears in the community feed with the original link, your comment, and a badge showing where it came from. Others can view the original post too!
